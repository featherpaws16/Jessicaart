Welcome!

After years of dreaming what it would be like to have time to draw and paint again, it finally hit me that the only one standing in my way was me. I had the time, I was just finding excuses to not do what I love. Then something just clicked, the desire to do, to create, it was on like a fire in my belly. And so I started this year, and I am in love with the process from start to finish. Whether it be sketching, creating illustrations, turning photos of your fur babies into art... I love it and I hope that love is conveyed in my art. So when I am not at my normal 9 to 5 or attending to the many other duties of being mom to very busy little people, you can bet your paintbrush that I will be somewhere creating. I look forward to sharing this journey with you, creating for and with you, and having a heck of a lot of fun doing it!
